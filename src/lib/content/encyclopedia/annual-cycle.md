# Annual Cycle

The society operates on a one-year governing cycle. Each November, the membership votes on any standing referenda and the next General Assembly is drawn by sortition from eligible members. The newly drawn Assembly takes its seat with a clear mandate: the referendum results set the society's direction for the coming year, and the Assembly is charged with carrying that mandate out.

The fixed cycle ensures that authority turns over regularly, that no assembly accumulates tenure beyond its term, and that the membership's will is tested and renewed annually.
