# New Member Onboarding

Use this guide when welcoming someone into the society.

## 1) Intake Interview

- Review basic needs and constraints.
- Confirm emergency contacts.
- Capture skills and preferred contribution areas.

## 2) Orientation Session

- Explain charter commitments and participation expectations.
- Walk through messaging, events, and profile setup.
- Introduce local points of contact.

## 3) First-Week Follow-up

- Verify access to needed tools and spaces.
- Confirm calendar enrollment and activity assignments.
- Record any blockers for officer review.
